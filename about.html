About St. Catherine Junior School

Building character, nurturing minds, shaping futures

Our History & Mission

A legacy of excellence in education spanning decades

1985

Foundation

St. Catherine Junior School was established with a vision to provide quality education to young minds in the community.

1992

First Expansion

Added new classrooms and library facilities to accommodate growing student population.

2005

Technology Integration

Introduced computer laboratory and digital learning resources to enhance modern education.

2015

Accreditation

Received national recognition for excellence in Junior education and student development.

2024

Modern Era

Continuing to innovate with new teaching methods and facilities for holistic student development.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our educational approach

Respect

We foster mutual respect among students, teachers, and the entire school community.

Discipline

We instill self-discipline and responsibility in our students for their personal growth.

Teamwork

We encourage collaboration and teamwork to achieve common goals and objectives.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all aspects of education and character development.

Message from Our Head Teacher
