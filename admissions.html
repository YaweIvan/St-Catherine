Admission Guidelines

Everything you need to know about joining St. Catherine Junior School

Required Documents

Birth Certificate

Original and photocopy required for age verification

Passport Photos

4 recent passport-size photographs

Previous School Report

Academic records from previous institution (if applicable)

Medical Certificate

Health clearance from certified medical practitioner

Parent/Guardian ID

Copy of national ID or passport

Immunization Card

Complete vaccination records

Application Process

Simple steps to secure your child's place at our school

1

Download Form

Download and complete the application form with accurate information

2

3

Admission

Fee Structure

Transparent and affordable fee structure for quality education

Class Level Tuition Fee (Per Term) Development Fee (Annual) Total (Per Term)
Pre-Junior UGX 150,000 UGX 50,000 UGX 200,000
Junior 1-3 UGX 180,000 UGX 60,000 UGX 240,000

Additional Fees:

  • Lunch: UGX 30,000 per term (optional)
  • Transport: UGX 40,000 per term (optional)
